Madhya Pradesh: Culture & Heritage

Traditional Dress

  • Men: Dhoti, Safa (turban), Mirzai, Bandi (jacket)
  • Women: Lehenga-Choli, Odhni, Chanderi and Maheshwari sarees, Bandhani sarees
  • Traditional jewelry includes pajeb (anklet), bangles, necklaces, and other silver/gold ornaments[web:1][web:2].

Traditional Food

  • Poha-Jalebi: A popular breakfast combination, with flattened rice and crispy sweet jalebi[web:10].
  • Dal Bafla: Baked wheat balls dipped in ghee, served with spicy dal[web:14][web:6].
  • Bhutte Ka Kees: Grated corn cooked with spices and milk, monsoon delicacy[web:6].
  • Sabudana Khichdi: Tapioca pearls with peanuts and spices, enjoyed during festivals[web:6].
  • Gosht Korma & Seekh Kebabs: Show Mughal influences in regional cuisine[web:8].

Festivals

  • Khajuraho Dance Festival: Celebrates Indian classical dance forms at the iconic temple site[web:7].
  • Bhagoria Haat: The tribal “festival of love” known for unique elopement tradition[web:7].
  • Malwa Utsav: Spectacular art and dance event in Indore and Ujjain[web:7].
  • Diwali & Holi: Major festivals celebrated with lights, colors, music, and local treats[web:9].
  • Pachmarhi Utsav: Hill station fair with folk dance, music, and crafts[web:9].
